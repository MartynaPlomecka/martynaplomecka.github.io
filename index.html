Martyna Plomecka

I am a Senior Researcher at Microsoft Research Redmond working toward universal information retrieval and recommender systems with large language models. I graduated from the University of Michigan in May 2022 with a PhD in computer science working with Danai Koutra on text-augmented graph learning.
